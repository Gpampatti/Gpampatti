About Us

Something about us...

Bhartiya Krushi Kendra was established in the year 2005 in Ahmadnagar, Maharashtra. We are the Supplier, Distributor, Wholesaler and Trader of Organic Pesticides, Bio-Pesticides, Maize Seed, Sesame Seeds, Cotton Seeds, etc. India is an agriculture economy unlike other developed countries, which are industrial economy. That means the economy of the country depends, to a large extent, on agriculture. Crop cultivation sustains almost two-third of the Indian population.Given the importance of agriculture, the productivity should be maintained to a bare minimum level or constantly improved to meet the ever-increasing demands.
